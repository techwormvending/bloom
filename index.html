What is Bloom?

Fresh Food,
Simplified.

Bloom sources and supplies fresh, quality food to smart vending locations across Pittsburgh. We partner with vending operators to provide daily-refreshed inventory that keeps customers happy and locations profitable.

Our focus is simple: source products your customers want, deliver them fresh the same day, and make restocking effortless. We work with AI-powered vending systems to optimize logistics and minimize waste.

Everything we supply is fresh. We emphasize organic and natural products where quality and pricing make sense — premium selection without the premium price tag on everything.

80%+ of our products are organic or sourced from natural suppliers. The remaining 20% are carefully curated premium items that deliver exceptional value.
