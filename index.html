What is Bloom?

Bloom sources and supplies fresh, quality food to smart vending locations across Pittsburgh.

We believe fresh food shouldn't be complicated. Bloom partners with vending operators to provide daily-refreshed inventory that keeps customers happy and locations profitable.

Our focus is simple: source products your customers want, deliver them fresh the same day, and make restocking effortless for location managers.

We work with AI-powered vending systems to optimize logistics and inventory management. This means your products stay fresher longer, waste is minimized, and your machines are always stocked with what sells. It's the perfect pairing: premium fresh food + intelligent vending technology.

Everything we supply is fresh. We emphasize organic and natural products where quality and pricing make sense, but we're honest about what we offer‚Äîpremium selection without the premium price tag on everything.

80%+ of our products are organic or sourced from natural suppliers. The remaining 20% are carefully curated premium items that deliver exceptional value.
